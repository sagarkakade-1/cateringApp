Order Management Module

This module will handle both Full Catering and Half Catering orders. Features will include order creation, status tracking, customer assignment, employee allocation, and inventory management per order.

Full Catering Orders
  • Complete service package
  • Cook, waiter, service boys
  • Utensils, tables, transport
  • Water cans and equipment
Half Catering Orders
  • Custom item selection
  • Flexible service options
  • Partial equipment rental
  • Customizable packages
